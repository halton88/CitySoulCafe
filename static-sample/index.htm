Come join the City Soul Team every Wednesday for a night of music, spoken word, fun and memories you can't get anywhere else! DJ Supreme will be in the building providing the musical backdrop!

Our mic is open for poets, singers, comedians and musicians. First time performing? Come through! We have something special for all our new perfomers to welcome you to the family.
